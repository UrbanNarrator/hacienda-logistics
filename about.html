About Hacienda Logistics

Your trusted partner for transport and logistics solutions in East Africa

Our Story

Hacienda Logistics Ltd is a privately owned transport company formed with the long-term view to be the best Transportation Company in East Africa. We have been in operation for over 8 years, providing comprehensive Transport and Logistics solutions to clients across the region.

Based in Embakasi, off North Airport road, opposite Africa Global Logistics (AGL), we have strategically positioned ourselves to serve the growing logistics needs of Kenya and East Africa. Our location provides easy access to major transport routes and the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport.

Since our inception, we have built a reputation for reliability, innovation, and excellence in service delivery. Our commitment to quality has enabled us to establish long-lasting relationships with major clients across various industries.
